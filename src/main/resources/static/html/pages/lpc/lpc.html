License Proficiency Check (LPC)
Revalidation & Renewal

To maintain a current Pilot License, pilots are required to do regular training, but more importantly, a good pilot needs to stay current on the necessary skills and knowledge to operate an aircraft safely within the demands of commercial aviation.

Why Choose Our LPC Revalidation & Renewal Services?

Continuous Improvement: Evaluates a pilot’s ability to handle routine and unexpected situations, promoting a culture of continuous improvement and preparedness for any scenario.

Industry Safety: Contributes to maintaining a high level of safety within the aviation industry by systematically assessing and reinforcing a pilot’s skills.

Compliance and Proficiency: Ensures compliance with industry standards, guaranteeing that pilots remain proficient, adaptable, and capable of handling the dynamic challenges of commercial aviation.

Available Aircraft: At Scandinavian Training Academy, we offer License Proficiency Checks on B737, B787, and A320.
